They made food for them out in the field at Bavnhöjen in Rold when they came back from riding the summer in the town. But once the mound folk who lived in the mound were offended by them. It was just like the mound was going to be tipped over on them, and the cooks’ pots got tipped over and they ran down off the mound, while their food and pots came tumbling down behind them. After that they didn’t make food out in the open in the field.
