At that time, they burned a lot of charcoal and sold that and the tree ash in Ålborg. Kræn Hyldgård once got 24 shillings for a bushel. When he came home, a neighbor said, ”How much did you get?” – “I didn’t get more than 24 shillings.” Yeah, that was far too little, he’d gotten two marks. “Well, then you must be quite happy, brother, that all that you had was ash.” That was a good answer, since he knew well that he was trying to feed him a lie.
