A farmhand and a hired boy shared a room, but the farmhand was always tormented by nightmares. So one evening he bored a hole all the way through the door post and say to the boy: "When it gets on top of me, you are to jump quickly up and jam this stick in the hole," and with that he gave him a stopper, he had cut it so that it just fit the hole. When it came again at night, the boy did just as the farmhand had told him to. They lit a candle and they saw that there was a naked lady standing in the middle of the room. When they pulled the stick out of the door post, she disappeared.
