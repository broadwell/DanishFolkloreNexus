They rode the nags into the woods at night. Jens Östergård from Ersted went out one morning to find his nag, but the boy hadn’t set them out where he’d asked them to, and he couldn’t find the nag. Now Jens Östergård got quite worked up, and Trude-Niels, who was out in the barn threshing, heard him say, “Satan should break your necks into a thousand pieces, you dog-headed bastards!”
